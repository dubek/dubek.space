KVM Forum 2021 talks on Confidential Computing

Posted on 2021, Sep 29

As part of work done at IBM Research on Confidential Computing (specifically with AMD SEV), I participated in two talks presented in KVM Forum 2021.

The first one deals with securing the boot process of memory-encrypted Linux VMs. It describes changes we contirbuted to OVMF and QEMU. [youtube | slides]

The second talk describes the challenges of live migration in SEV and SEV-ES guests and our attempts to tackle them. This is still work-in-progress. [youtube | slides]
